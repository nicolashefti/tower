Getting started

Tower helps you follow easily small ads by converting the search result page into a RSS feed you can subscribe and passively follow.

Let's take an exampe: someone stolle you "Dahon" bike...

Get the search page URL

Go to http://2memain.be and search for "Dahon". Copy the URL of the result page, in our case: http://www.2ememain.be/marche/2/dahon/?locale=all

Give it to tower and subscribe to the RSS feed

Paste the URL in the form on the right hand side. You will be redirected to a RSS feed page containing the result of your Dahon research. You can now subscribe to this feed and get passively the new small ads coming. Maybe you'll find your bike again!

Tower is an Open Source project developed by Nicolas Hefti. Code source available on Github.
